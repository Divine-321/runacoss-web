Courses We Offer

We offer three major courses in our Computer Science program, designed to equip you with cutting-edge skills and knowledge

Cybersecurity

Learn to protect computer systems, networks, and data from cyber threats with our comprehensive Cybersecurity course. Covering security fundamentals, risk management, encryption, secure coding, penetration testing, and incident response, this course prepares you for a career in this in-demand field.
Gain a deep understanding of cybersecurity and prepare to defend against cyber threats in this critical field.

Information Technology

Learn to design, develop, and manage computer-based systems and technologies with our comprehensive Information Technology course. Covering IT fundamentals, software applications, data management, and networking, this course prepares you for a career in this dynamic field.
Gain a broad understanding of information technology and prepare to succeed in this rapidly evolving field.

Computer Science

Explore the fascinating world of computer science and learn to design, develop, and apply computer systems and technologies with our comprehensive course. Covering the fundamentals of computer science, programming, data structures, algorithms, software engineering, and computer systems, this course prepares you for a career in this cutting-edge field.
Gain a deep understanding of computer science and prepare to innovate and shape the future of technology.

Our Curriculum

The Computer Science department curriculum is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in the principles and practices of computer science, preparing them for successful careers in this dynamic and rapidly evolving field

Lectures

Here, you'll find a comprehensive collection of theoretical lectures on various topics in Computer Science. Our lectures cover a wide range of topics, including programming languages, data structures, algorithms, computer systems, and software engineering. Each lecture is carefully crafted to provide a clear and concise understanding of the subject matter. Our lectures are perfect for students, professionals, and anyone looking to expand their knowledge in the field. We provide a solid foundation in the principles and concepts of computer science.

Practicals

Here, you'll find a collection of hands-on exercises and projects to apply theoretical concepts to real-world problems. Our practicals cover a range of topics, including programming, data analysis, web development, artificial intelligence, and database management. Each practical includes a clear description of the problem, requirements, and expected output. We provide necessary resources and references to help you develop the skills and expertise needed to succeed. Our practicals are designed to help you succeed in the field of computer science.

Internship Training(IT)

We offer a comprehensive 6-month internship training program in collaboration with top IT companies. Our program provides students with practical experience in various computer science fields, including software development, data science, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. Students work on real-world projects, collaborate with industry professionals, and develop skills and expertise. Our partner companies include leading IT firms, startups, and innovative organizations, ensuring a dynamic and diverse range of settings.
